How i survive Quarantine.

Quarantine made a long holiday. At first, it might be fun, no school and no homework. By the time flew, i felt bored and i missed my friends so much.
At the 12th week of Quarantine, my friends send me their handmade cookies and foods from their home. It was realy nice and i had an idea in my mind.
I made them some acrylic keychains and some cookies to their house, they were so happy!.
From my friends, i heard alot of online classes. So i joined one, it's Generation Girl. I had great time in there! I learned about backend, coding, making a website, and more!
The mentors are realy nice, they provide bahasa and english class to!
I do things that i love, like painting, cooking, and more.
Technology is grows really good this times. If you miss your friends, you can video call them, if you ran out of money, you can start to open an online store.
Quarantine might be boring, but thats okay, a lot of things to do. I didnt have my graduation party because of this pandemic. so we have it in Zoom(video call).
Stay save, stay home, stay productive.
